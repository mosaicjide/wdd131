About the Competition

The Good Men Cycling Competition is an annual event held every November to promote healthy living and community bonding. Organized by the Good Men Association of Nigeria, it brings together cycling enthusiasts from across the nation.

Our Vision

To become the leading community cycling competition in Africa, fostering unity, fitness, and sportsmanship.

Our Mission

To create a fun, safe, and challenging environment for amateur and professional cyclists alike, while building awareness for health and social causes.

Why Join Us?

  • Exciting routes through scenic Nigerian landscapes
  • Chance to meet top cycling enthusiasts and professionals
  • Great prizes and recognition
  • Promote health and fitness within the community
