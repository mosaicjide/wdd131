Good Men Cycling Competition - Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Good Men Cycling Competition

This name reflects the cycling competition organized by the Good Men Association of Nigeria. It is clear, descriptive, and tailored to the nature of the event, ensuring recognition and branding consistency.

Optional domain availability: goodmencycling.org

Site Purpose

The website serves as the official hub for the Good Men Cycling Competition, providing details about the event, registration information, past event highlights, contact forms, and event countdown. It connects participants, spectators, and organizers.

Scenarios

  • How do I register for the next cycling competition?
  • Where can I see photos and results from previous events?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color (#2a9d8f): Used for headings, buttons, and accent elements.
  • Secondary Color (#264653): Used for footer, navigation hover effects, and borders.

Typography

  • Font: Segoe UI, sans-serif
  • Headings: Bold Segoe UI
  • Body Text: Regular Segoe UI
